Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to share with the platform through uploads, form submissions, or API requests. This may include artist, artwork, or sales data that you explicitly provide.

All data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized team members. We employ standard security practices to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your information.
